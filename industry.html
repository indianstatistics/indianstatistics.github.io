Scripts to read unit-level ASI data

The CAS Programme of the Centre for Economic Studies and Planning, JNU has supported an initiative to produce Free and Open-source scripts to read ASI unit-level data and produce annotated databases out of them. The programmes are released under GNU GPL license. We are hosting these programmes for use by anyone who may be interested.

Go here to get the scripts.
