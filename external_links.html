Links to other online sources of data

Major online sources of socio-economic data on India are:

RBI’s Database on Indian Economy

RBI’s database covers various aspects of economy and provides an easy interface for obtaining data.

Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation

Contains:
  • Reports of the surveys conducted by the National Sample Survey Organisation
  • Statistics released by the Central Statistical Organisation
    • National Account Statistics
    • Annual Survey of Industries
    • Index of Industrial Production
    • Price indices
    • Energy statistics

Census of India
