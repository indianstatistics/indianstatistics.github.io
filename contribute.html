Why contribute data to indianstatistics.org

  • indianstatistics.org is a non-commercial initiative and hopes to build a database for public use through contributions of scholars.
  • indianstatistics.org will cite and acknowledge your contribution.
  • Data given to indianstatistics.org will be released free of cost for unrestricted public use.
  • indianstatistics.org will ensure security of data provided to us.

What kind of data do we accept

  • We welcome contributions of
    • relevant public data with clearly specified citation.
    • relevant primary data owned by you which you are willing to give to indianstatistics.org for release under a Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 Unported License.
  • You may provide data pertaining to variables on which data do not exist on indianstatistics.org or provide updates to data that already exist on indianstatistics.org.
  • Needless to say, we will cite and acknowledge all contributors of data.

How to contribute

  • Email to admin at indianstatistics.org with details of the data that you would like to contribute. Please describe the dataset, and tell us about the source and copyrights. Also indicate if you would be willing to take the responsibility of providing future updates to the dataset.
  • If possible, provide us with a sample subset of data.
  • We would evaluate the dataset in terms of its relevance to the scope of indianstatistics.org, accuracy of information contained, and for issues of copyright.
  • If we can accept the dataset, we will get back with the details of the format in which the data should be provided.
  • It will be useful if you can make the effort of putting the data in the desired format.
