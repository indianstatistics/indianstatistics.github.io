Code of Conduct

  1. It is mandatory for all the participants to stay for the entire duration of the school. They would not be allowed to miss any class or activity.
  2. Participants cannot deny to do their share of the collective chores (cleaning, washing, laundry, kitchen work, etc) as part of the working groups.
  3. The participants need to adhere to timing of the school - for classes, meals, collective work etc.
  4. Participants should not leave the school premises, unless necessary, and that too only with the consent of the school organisers.
  5. Participants are expected to clean their rooms and wash their own clothes.
  6. Personal boundaries of individuals should be respected.
  7. Any cases of sexual harassment will be dealt with strictly and may include expulsion from the school.
  8. Any form of discrimination based on race, religion, caste, gender, disability, etc. will not be tolerated. Persons who faces such discrimination are encouraged to report it to the school organisers. Such behaviour may be dealt through discussion, warning and expulsion based on severity of the incident.
  9. The School is an alcohol-free zone. Consumption or possession of alcohol by participants in the school is strictly prohibited.
  10. Participants are strictly prohibited to bring or use any kind of drugs or narcotics (including marijuana) at the school.
  11. Participants of different ages and differing levels of experience will attend the school. There cannot be any hierarchy, and everyone will get equal preference in voicing their opinions and participation in activities. All the participants need to do an equal share of collective work (cleaning, washing, kitchen work and others), irrespective of their organisational stature and age.
  12. Participants will have to take care of safety of their own valuable goods. Organisers will not be responsible if the items are lost or misplaced.
  13. Participants are encouraged to bring pamphlets/materials that give an idea of the work their organisations are doing, to share with the rest of the participants.
